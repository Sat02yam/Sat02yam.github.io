CONCEPT

Podium is a review taking platform which aims to get the best reviews to the company who provided services and link the same to the Google review and brings out the most positive and genuine reviews to the company and helps them to grow!

02

PROCESS

My design process was like
-RESEARCH
Took a considerable time in thinking about the requirments of the client and their viewpoint on the project.
-IDEATION
Ideated many ideas from the reference designs and noted down all the relevent things which I can include on my design.
-DESIGN
For the design part I took a pencil and paper for Low-fed Designs and picked Figma after wards for the high-Fed design.
-FEEDBACK
After this designing part is completed I took my design to various business holders who uses google maps and focus on the reviews by customers on the maps for their business; Explained them the Idea and took their reviews and with the help of prototype enabled them experience the flow and noted their reviews.
